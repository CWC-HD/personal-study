About Us


Experience a different kind of nature in a small world, where holidays are filled with laughter and new experiences to explore and learn.

Mini World Fantasyland Insect Kingdom Resort

Having mastered the technology of shrinking and discovered the wonderful insect kingdom, we have entered into a partnership with the insect kingdom to build a resort to which humans will be invited. The Mini World will host a number of events each season, inviting visitors from the human world. The events will be organised by the Ant Tribe, the Bee Tribe, the Butterfly Tribe, the Dragonfly Tribe and the Beetle Tribe. Each tribe will be doing different activities and you can participate in as many activities as you like during your stay at the resort. We are still working on improving the programme, so if you are interested you can join our team!
