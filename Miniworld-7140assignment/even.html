Ant Tribest

Experience a tense and exciting game of snatch and grab and be rewarded with unexpected prizes for your participation.

Camps

Choose the Black Ant or Red Ant camp to participate in the ant nest attack and defense battle. During the event, both sides will take resources from the other side's camp and put them into their own camp.

Rules

The camp that can get more resources from the other camp within the time limit wins. The winning side will be awarded a title by the queen, but only in the ant tribe.

Honeycomb

EA bee theme park where honey lovers can passionately sample a variety of bee delicacies and maintenance items.

Tour

We will visit the production process of "Sweet Water", the best-selling drink in mini world. "Sweet Water" is a very popular drink in mini world, and its source is the factory in Honeybee Park, so let's take a tour.

Meal

Dine in the honey-themed restaurant, where all the food is made with honey from the bee park, and Chef B will provide you with a delicious dinner!

Maintenance

Did you know that honey is a wonderful maintenance product and that bathing in honey has unexpected benefits? Come to the theme park and enjoy a honey bath and spa at night!

Butterfly dances

A ball on the pistil, under the stars with all the beautiful and handsome butterfly girls and gentlemen

Place

The flowers under the stars will be used as a dance floor, so you and your partner can move from flower to flower, and if you're lucky, you might find a nighttime bloom of nightshade.

Ball

The event features several butterfly girls and gentlemen, the Eye Butterfly, Yellow Butterfly, Rattle Butterfly and Grey Butterfly. They are extremely rare butterflies that fly at night and they are willing to dance with you at night.
